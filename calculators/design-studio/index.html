💡 How to use 2D & 3D modes

🗺 2D Mode

  • Tap any panel on the map to add or remove it individually. Grey outlines are inactive panels you can click to activate.
  • Drag the slider to quick-select the top N highest-producing panels.
  • Edit segment angles in the Panel Layout panel — type a new tilt or azimuth and hit Apply to rotate every panel in that segment.
  • Switch to Ground Mount to manually click-place panels on land, drag to reposition, or duplicate rows/columns.
  • Draw obstructions to mark chimneys, vents, or skylights — panels inside will be excluded from production.

🌍 3D Mode

  • Click N / E / S / W in the top-right corner to orbit the house at a 45° angle from each compass direction.
  • Drag the view to rotate · scroll to zoom · right-click drag to pan the camera.
  • Click any panel in 3D to add or remove it — same as 2D mode. Inactive panels show as faint outlines you can re-enable.
  • Auto-loads first when you pick an address. Falls back to 2D if Google Photorealistic 3D Tiles aren't available for the location.
  • Click "🗺 Back to 2D" to switch to flat editing mode — your choice is remembered for future addresses.
