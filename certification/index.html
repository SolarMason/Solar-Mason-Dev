Our Credentials

Solar Mason maintains all required state and federal licenses, certifications, and insurance to deliver professional solar installations throughout Pennsylvania. Our commitment to ongoing education ensures our team stays current with evolving codes, technologies, and best practices.

🏗️ HIC-181628

Pennsylvania Home Improvement Contractor license — required for all residential solar installations in the Commonwealth.

⚡ CON-9505

Pennsylvania General Contractor license for commercial construction projects including ground-mount and utility-scale solar arrays.

📋 NABCEP Aligned

Our engineering and installation practices align with North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners (NABCEP) standards.

🛡️ Fully Insured

Comprehensive general liability, workers' compensation, and professional liability coverage protects our clients and team throughout every project.

Manufacturer Certifications

Solar Mason is a certified installer for Q CELLS, Enphase Energy, SolarEdge, IronRidge, and SEMS. These manufacturer partnerships ensure proper installation techniques, warranty compliance, and access to premium equipment at competitive pricing.
